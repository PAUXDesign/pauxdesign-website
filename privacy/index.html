Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 April 2026

1. Who I am

Paux Design is a freelance product design service based in London, UK. This policy explains how personal data is handled on pauxdesign.uk.

2. Data I collect

  • Contact details you submit through forms (name, email, company, and message).
  • Project details you choose to share in enquiry messages.
  • Basic technical data required for website operation and security (for example browser/device context collected by hosting providers).

3. How I use your data

  • To respond to project enquiries.
  • To provide proposals, communicate project updates, and deliver services.
  • To protect the website against abuse or fraudulent activity.

4. Legal basis

Data is processed based on legitimate business interest, pre-contract communication at your request, and legal obligations where required.

5. Data sharing

Your data is not sold. It may be processed by trusted service providers strictly for business operations (such as form delivery, email, or hosting).

6. Data retention

Personal data is kept only as long as necessary for communication, service delivery, and legal or accounting requirements.

7. Your rights

You may request access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection, or portability of your personal data, subject to applicable law.

8. Contact

For privacy requests, contact: contact@pauxdesign.uk.
